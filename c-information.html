The Usual Suspects - EU Softcore PvE/PvP Guild

Prepare to be astonished, as what you're about to discover may seem too good to be true. Nevertheless, there are compelling reasons for you to give it a chance, and you might find yourself pleasantly surprised. We're on a mission to become the premier Softcore guild within the vast World of Warcraft Universe (tongue-in-cheek, of course), where the focus is on genuine gaming enjoyment rather than constant rushing and relentless criticism. If you're tired of hearing phrases like "Why are you still at level 20? Speed up, dude!" or "Why are you using that? You should use this, you [expletive]...", then our guild is a breath of fresh air. Are you intrigued? You should be! If you share our vision of a guild where fun takes precedence, feel free to explore your journey with us further. Take the next step by checking out our Application Form. and Recruitment pages.
